How ZimKAG analyses your contract

A three-stage pipeline combining a fine-tuned language model with domain knowledge.

1

Document parsing

PDF, DOCX or TXT is parsed and segmented into individual clauses using a heading-aware splitter trained on Zimbabwean construction contracts.

2

Legal-BERT classification

Each clause is classified into high · medium · low · opportunity · neutral by a Legal-BERT model fine-tuned on a 10 000-clause dataset (JCT, NEC4, FIDIC, bespoke ZW).

3

Knowledge-graph guidance

A Zimbabwe-aware knowledge graph + semantic retrieval suggests fairer rewrites, escalates risk where appropriate, and flags currency, indemnity and penalty traps.
